Footwear Policy

All sales are final. There are no returns or exchanges on footwear.

Return Policy

Kids Of Immigrants will happily grant a return for online store credit only. We unfortunately do not offer reimbursement for the original shipping cost and you will be solely responsible for the returned item(s) shipping cost. The following requirements must also be met:

  • I. Item(s) are within 14 days of delivery date
  • II. Item(s) are perfect, unworn in its new condition with all tags still attached.
  • III. Items(s) are not “Final Sale” or “LOVE Bundle” products
